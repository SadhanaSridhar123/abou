The Story of One Direction

The band first fromed in London, England when all the boys auditioned for the X factor. They first auditioned as soloists. However the judges, especially Simon Cowell, thought they were too good to be eliminated so a band named One Direction was formed.


One Direction, often shortened to 1D, are an English-Irish pop boy band formed in London, England in 2010. The group are composed of Niall Horan, Liam Payne, Harry Styles, Louis Tomlinson, and previously Zayn Malik until his departure from the group in March 2015.
